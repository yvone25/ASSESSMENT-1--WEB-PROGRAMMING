Plot Overview

The movie *Scream* follows a high school student named Sidney Prescott who becomes the target of a mysterious killer known as Ghostface. The killer stalks her and her friends, leaving behind a trail of gruesome murders. As the body count rises, Sidney, along with her friends and law enforcement, try to uncover the identity of the killer. Throughout the film, there are many twists and turns, including the revelation that the killer may be someone from Sidney's past.

The movie is a modern twist on the slasher genre, with the killer often engaging in meta-references to horror movie tropes and conventions, creating a unique blend of tension and dark humor. As Sidney uncovers the mystery, she must confront her fears and face the truth about her family's dark secrets.

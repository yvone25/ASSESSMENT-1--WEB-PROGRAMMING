Scream (1996)

The iconic slasher that redefined horror.

Plot Overview

In the quiet town of Woodsboro, a masked killer known as Ghostface begins a deadly game of terror. High school student Sidney Prescott and her friends become targets as the mysterious figure stalks them, blending slasher horror with self-aware humor and clever twists.

Key Information

  • Release Date: December 20, 1996
  • Director: Wes Craven
  • Writer: Kevin Williamson
  • Genre: Horror / Slasher
  • Runtime: 111 minutes

Cast & Creative Team

  • Neve Campbell as Sidney Prescott
  • Courteney Cox as Gale Weathers
  • David Arquette as Dewey Riley
  • Matthew Lillard as Stu Macher
  • Skeet Ulrich as Billy Loomis
  • Directed by Wes Craven

Main Characters

  • Sidney Prescott: The smart, resilient final girl.
  • Gale Weathers: Ambitious reporter chasing the story.
  • Dewey Riley: The lovable small-town deputy.
  • Ghostface: The mysterious masked killer.

Reviews

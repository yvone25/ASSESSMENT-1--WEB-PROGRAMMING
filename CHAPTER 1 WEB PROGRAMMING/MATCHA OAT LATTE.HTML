Iced Matcha Oat Latte

The Iced Matcha Oat Latte is a refreshing and creamy drink perfect for warm days. Made with high-quality matcha powder, oat milk, and a touch of sweetness, this latte is both energizing and dairy-free.

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This Iced Matcha Oat Latte is the perfect balance of earthy and sweet, offering a refreshing boost of energy without the jitters of traditional coffee. The smooth, creamy oat milk complements the rich flavor of matcha, making it an indulgent yet healthy treat. Plus, it's a great option for those looking for a dairy-free and vegan-friendly alternative to regular lattes. Whether you need a pick-me-up in the morning or a cool drink to unwind during the day, this recipe will leave you feeling refreshed and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoon matcha powder (ceremonial grade)
  • 2 tablespoons warm water
  • 1 teaspoon sweetener (honey, maple syrup, or sugar) (optional)
  • 1 cup oat milk (barista blend recommended)
  • Ice cubes

Method

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a small bowl to remove any clumps.
  2. Add warm water and whisk vigorously in a W motion until smooth and frothy.
  3. Stir in your sweetener if desired.
  4. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  5. Pour oat milk over the ice.
  6. Slowly pour the matcha mixture over the oat milk for a layered effect.
  7. Stir gently before drinking or enjoy as is!
